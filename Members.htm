Jiyeon Lee
M.S,Chemical and Biological Engineering

Jiyeon, Pd.D. student at University of Florida and a menber of Dr. Lele's group,received her B.S degree in Chemical Engineering and M. S. degree in Chemical and Biological Engineering from Seoul National University,Korea.Her post graduation research was about nano patterning, electronic materials and organic devices, specifically studying modification and application of electronic materials.Her current research interests are material-based cell mechanism and application of nanotechnology to cell kinetics. Also her current research includes study of cell behaviors on various substrates, such as nanorods, polyacrylamide, and glass. This research will be expanded to study of cell migration on ECM in the future.
